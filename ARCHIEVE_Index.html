August 2022 - Plans for Finneon City were made.

September 2022 - Begin of Finneon City.

December 14th 2022 - Finneon city was merged with USF, Unnamed Canada and Unnamed Mexico.

December 15th 2022 - Unnamed Canada and Unnamed Mexico were changed to Emilitronië and Armenistan and Cum Islands were going to be made.

December 27th 2022 - Site created.

January 2nd 2023 - Construction of the Uma Bridge has began. (Finneon City)

January 3rd 2023 - Uma Bridge is finished. (Finneon City)

January 20th 2023 - KORTZ-Building officially opened. (Mine York City)

February 6th 2023 - RedLight City is build on the east coast.

February 7th 2023 - The Tittie Theater has been build. (RedLight City)

April 26th 2023 - Crusoë is founded and is the most Southern place at the moment. (Peninsula of Armenistan)

May 12th 2023 - A lot of smaller projects were finished. Starting today, the highway between Finneon City and RedLight City is available. It goes through the newest addition to Finneon City: Kinomo Kingdom (きのも)

May 28th 2023 - Began construction of the Queen's Passage.

May 29th 2023 - San Finncisco is finnished.

May 30th 2023 - Daniël and Camille joined.

June 11th 2023 - The first Nether Highway Line is built from san Finncisco to the Queen's Passage.
